Sanbao Receipts

A lightweight agent identity and execution receipt interface for transparent, auditable autonomous actions.

Agent Identity Receipts Audit Trail

Agent

Problem

Agents can act, but users still lack a simple way to inspect who acted, what was executed, whether the action was authorized, and what outcome was produced.

Sanbao Receipts turns agent actions into structured receipts that can later map to ERC-8004 identity, delegated permissions, payment flows, and onchain attestations.

Execution Receipts

Human-readable receipts for autonomous actions.

Why it matters

  • Reduce ambiguity in agent execution
  • Give humans a compact verification surface
  • Prepare for wallet-linked and onchain accountability
  • Make autonomous systems easier to trust and debug
